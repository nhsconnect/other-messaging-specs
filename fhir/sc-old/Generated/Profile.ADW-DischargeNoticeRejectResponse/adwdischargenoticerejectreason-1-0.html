    ADW Discharge Notice Reject Reason-1-0

    Summary

    Name Description
    Defining URL: http://fhir.nhs.net/ValueSet/adw-discharge-notice-reject-reason-1-0
    Name: ADW Discharge Notice Reject Reason-1-0
    Definition: A vocabulary that identifies the reason for disgreeing with a Discharge Notice
    Status: Active
    Last Updated: 13/07/2016 09:00:00 +00:00

    This valueset includes all the codes for ADW Discharge Notice Reject Reason-1-0:

    Code Display
    SSNR Social Services cannot confirm agreement to the discharge date but the planned services are being arranged. I will confirm closer to the discharge date.
    SSNA Social Services cannot confirm agreement to the planned services and so cannot agree to the discharge date.
    APNC Social Services cannot confirm agreement to the discharge date as the assessment process has not been completed.
    PFNA Social Services cannot confirm agreement to the discharge date as the patient/family has not yet agreed to the planned services.
    NOTELIG Social Services cannot confirm agreement to the discharge date as the patient/family is not eligible for services funded by social services.
    NF Social Services cannot confirm agreement to the discharge date as public funding has not yet been agreed.
    NSTF Social Services do not agree that it is safe to discharge the patient on the stated date.
    SNA The previously arrange services to be provided by social services are not no longer available on discharge.
    PWC The person has withdrawn their consent to social services involvement.
    DOO Social services cannot confirm agreement to discharge date because awaiting services to be arranged
