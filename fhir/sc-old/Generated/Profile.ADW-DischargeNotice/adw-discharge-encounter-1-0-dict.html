    The patient who was admiited to the hospital inpatient stay.
    Control 1..1
    Must Support True
    Aliases patient
    Comments

    While the encounter is always about the patient, the patient may not actually be known in all contexts of use.

    Encounter.period(In Patient Stay Period)
    Definition

    The start and end time of the inpatient stay
    Control 1..1
    Type Period
    Must Support True
    Comments

    The start is the admission date.

    The end is discharge date. Note: the end date may be in the future to indicate when the patient is proposed to be discharged.

    Encounter.period.end
    Definition

    The end of the in patient stay (i.e the discharge time) . Note the end time may be in the future, which means that period is expected/planned to end at that time.
    Control 1..1 ?
    Type DateTime
    Must Support True
    Comments

    The high value includes any matching date/time. i.e. 2012-02-03T10:00:00 is in a period that has a end value of 2012-02-03.
