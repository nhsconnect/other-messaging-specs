Version 1.0 : DRAFT B

    The following structure definitions are available in this profile.

    NameTypeDescription & Constraints
    COFE-Accommodation-Observation-1ObservationThis Observation Resource regards the patient's accommodation.
    COFE-ADTRT-Observation-1ObservationAdvanced Decision to Refuse Treatment details.
    COFE-Advance-Statement-Observation-1ObservationObservation Resource to represent a text based advance statement.
    COFE-AllergicOrAdverseReactions-AllergyIntolerance-1AllergyIntoleranceRecords information of a known allergy or adverse reaction relating to the patient.
    COFE-Composition-End-Of-Life-1CompositionDocument Composition
    COFE-CrisisCarePlan-Observation-1ObservationAn observation associated with the patients Crisis Care Plan.
    COFE-Device-1DeviceThis Device profile is used to identify an authoring device or system where SDS is not appropriate or available. The organisation which owns or is responsible for the device or system will be identified using the organisation's ODS (NACS) code. An authoring device is normally described as a device that has processed some information and created an output without human involvement.
    COFE-Diagnosis-Condition-1ConditionRecord of any condition that the patient has been diagnosed with.
    COFE-DNACPRDecision-QuestionnaireResponse-1QuestionnaireResponseThis QuestionnaireResponse Resource carries answers to questions regarding a patients decision on cardio-pulmonary resuscitation.
    COFE-Document-Bundle-1BundleThis Bundle resource is constrained to represent a container to collect a combination of the COFE Document resources.
    COFE-DocumentParticipantUniversal-1PractitionerThis Practitioner resource represents a participant where information about the person and the organisation is required to be sent between systems.
    COFE-Encounter-1EncounterThis encounter resource in most but not all cases covers an episode of care.
    COFE-EndOfLifeCarePlan-CarePlan-1CarePlanThis Care Plan resource represents a patients End of Life Care Plan.
    COFE-Guardian-Organization-1OrganizationThis Organization resource details the organisation which is acting as the patient's guardian.
    COFE-GuardianIdentifier-Person-1PersonAn identifier for the patients guardian.
    COFE-Housing-Observation-1ObservationObservation resource used to capture patient accommodation details.
    COFE-IssueOfAnticipatoryMeds-Procedure-1ProcedureThe record of issuing the anticipatory medicine box.
    COFE-LastingPowerofAttorney-Observation-1ObservationLasting power of attorney indicator.
    COFE-LegalStatus-Observation-1ObservationFinding of the legal status of the patient.
    COFE-Location-1LocationThis location resource represents a Health Care Facility
    COFE-MedicalDevices-Device-1DeviceThis Device Resource is to hold medical device information, when the medical device is not listed on the dictionary of medicines and devices (dm+d).
    COFE-MedicationOrder-1MedicationOrderThis MedicationOrder Resource holds details of medications that have been changed.
    COFE-MedicationsAdministered-MedicationStatement-1MedicationStatementMedicationStatement Resource to carry the current state of medications, or medical devices, following an episode of care.
    COFE-MedicationsAndMedicalDevicesSupplied-MedicationStatement-1MedicationStatementMedicationStatement Resource to carry the medications, or medical devices which are supplied following an episode of care.
    COFE-Mobility-Observation-1ObservationRecord of any mobility problems, either physical or other, that the person may have. This can include transport issues.
    COFE-OrganDonation-Observation-1ObservationTissue and Organ donation indicator.
    COFE-Organization-ODS-1OrganizationThis Organization resource details an organization registered with ODS.
    COFE-Organization-partOf-1OrganizationThis Organization resource carries information about the organisation the provider organisation is part of.
    COFE-Patient-1PatientThis Patient resource constrains the patient information supported by CDA to make it suitable for NHS use.
    COFE-PatientDeathInformation-Observation-1ObservationThis Observation resource represents information about the death of a patient or related to the death of a patient.
    COFE-PersonalCarePreference-Observation-1ObservationThis Observation Resource records the personal preferences of the patient relevant to the patients care.
    COFE-Practitioner-1PractitionerThis Practitioner resource represents an author where information about the person and the organisation they work for is required to be sent between systems.
    COFE-Prognosis-Observation-1ObservationThis Observation Resource represents a patients prognosis that is part of their crisis care plan.
    COFE-Provider-Organization-1OrganizationThis Organization resource carries information about the organisation which provides/provided care to the patient
    COFE-Related-Person-1RelatedPersonThis Related Person resource represents information for a person with a relationship with the patient
    COFE-Risk-Flag-1FlagThis Flag Resource represents risks. Risks include to self, to others, from others and clinical risks.
    COFE-Safeguarding-Observation-1ObservationSafeguarding issues indicator.
    COFE-Services-CarePlan-1CarePlanAny care provision or services that are currently being provided to the patient.
    COFE-SocialorPersonalCircumstances-Observation-1ObservationSocial and personal circumstances that the patient may have, including any details of dependants.
    COFE-SPMH-Observation-1ObservationDetails of the patients past medical, surgical and mental health history.
