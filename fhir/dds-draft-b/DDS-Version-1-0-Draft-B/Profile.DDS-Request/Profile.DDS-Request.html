    The following structure definitions are available in this profile.

    NameTypeDescription & Constraints
    DDS-Request-Health-Care-Professional-1-0PractitionerA Practitioner Resource constrained to provide a standard for a Diagnostic Data Services' health care professional.
    DDS-Request-Patient-1-0PatientThis patient resource is constrained to provide information about the patient who is the subject of the Diagnostic Data Services investigation, for example a Laboratory Service Report.
    DDS-Request-Specimen-1-0SpecimenBase StructureDefinition for Specimen Resource
    DDS-Request-1-0DiagnosticOrderThis DiagnosticOrder resource is constrained to provide information about the Diagnostic Data Services Request.

    No extensions are used within these resource constraints.
