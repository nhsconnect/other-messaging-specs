Version 1.0 : DRAFT B

    The following structure definitions are available in this profile.

    NameTypeDescription & Constraints
    DDS-Report-1-0DiagnosticReportA Diagnostic report - a combination of request information, atomic results, images, interpretation, as well as formatted reports.
    DDS-Report-Specimen-1-0SpecimenThis Specimen resource is constrained to represent a specimen suitable for use in Diagnostic Data Services.
    DDS-Report-Substance-1-0SubstanceThis Substance resource is constrained to represent substances in a Diagnostic Data Services context.
    DDS-Report-Patient-1-0PatientThis patient resource is constrained to provide information about the patient who is the subject of the Diagnostic Data Services investigation, for example a Laboratory Service Report.
    DDS-Report-Health-Care-Professional-1-0PractitionerA Practitioner Resource constrained to provide a standard for a Diagnostic Data Services' health care professional.
    DDS-Report-Organization-1-0OrganizationThis Organization resource is constrained to represent a healthcare organisation.
    DDS-Report-Device-1-0DeviceThe Device resource shows an instance of a manufactured item that is used in the provision of healthcare without being substantially changed through that activity. The device may be a medical or non-medical device.
    DDS-Report-Supporting-Information-Observation-1-0ObservationThis constrained FHIR observation resource is used to show any relevant supporting information relating to the subject of the observation.
    DDS-Report-Family-Member-History-1-0FamilyMemberHistoryThis resource records significant health events and conditions for a particular individual related to the subject. This resource represents a simple structure used to capture a family history for a particular family member.
    DDS-Report-Observation-1-0ObservationThis FHIR Observation resource is constrained to represent observations, such as results of laboratory and other tests in a Diagnostic Data Services context.

    No extensions are used within these resource constraints.
