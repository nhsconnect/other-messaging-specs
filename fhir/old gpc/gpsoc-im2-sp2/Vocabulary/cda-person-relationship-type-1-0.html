    CDA-Person-Relationship-Type-1-0

    Default Scheme: CDA Person Relationship Type

    Assigned OID: 2.16.840.1.113883.2.1.3.2.4.16.45

    System URI: http://fhir.nhs.net/ValueSet/cda-person-relationship-type-1-0

    Description

    A relationship between one person and another. These relationships are not necessarily mutually exclusive, for example a parent may be a child's next of kin.
