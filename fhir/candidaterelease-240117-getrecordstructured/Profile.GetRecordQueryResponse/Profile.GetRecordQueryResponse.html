Version 1.0.0 : Draft H

    The following structure definitions are available in this profile.

    NameTypeDescription & Constraints
    GPConnect-Searchset-Bundle-1BundleThis Bundle resource is constrained to represent a container to collect a combination of the GP Connect Query Response resources.
    GPConnect-OperationOutcome-1OperationOutcomeThe Operation Outcome resource supports a collection of error, warning or information messages that result from a GP Connect Query Request.
    GPConnect-Patient-1PatientThe Patient resource carries the patient demographic details.
    GPConnect-CareRecord-Composition-2CompositionThe Composition resource carries information in the patient's record.
    CareConnect-GPC-AllergyIntolerance-1AllergyIntoleranceCareConnect AllergyIntolerance profile, with further constraints applied to support the GP Connect requirements.
    CareConnect-GPC-Immunization-1ImmunizationCareConnect Immunization profile, with further constraints applied to support the GP Connect requirements.
    CareConnect-Medication-1MedicationCareConnect Medication profile.
    CareConnect-GPC-MedicationOrder-1MedicationOrderCareConnect MedicationOrder profile, with further constraints applied to support the GP Connect requirements.
    CareConnect-GPC-MedicationStatement-1MedicationStatementCareConnect MedicationStatement profile, with further constraints applied to support the GP Connect requirements.
    CareConnect-GPC-Condition-1ConditionUse to record detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician.
    GPConnect-Practitioner-1PractitionerThe Practitioner resource represents the healthcare professional directly or indirectly involved in the provision of healthcare related services.
    GPConnect-Location-1LocationThe Location resource provides information and details on the physical location and the services provided.
    GPConnect-Organization-1OrganizationThe Organization resource represents the organisation that employs the healthcare professional.
    GPConnect-Device-1DeviceThe Device resource represents a manufactured item that is used in the provision of healthcare related services.
