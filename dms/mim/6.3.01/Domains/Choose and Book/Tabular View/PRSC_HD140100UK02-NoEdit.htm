AppointmentCancellation

The Appointment Cancellation Response represents either a Confirmation or a Rejection of the request appointment cancellation sent from the Choose and Book  Service to the service provider.

A confirmation interaction confirms to the Choose and Book Service that the identified appointment has been cancelled by the service provider.

A rejection interaction informs the Choose and Book Service that the service provider is unable to cancel the identified appointment.

This identifier is unique within the Choose and Book  Service. It identifies the Unique Booking Reference Number (UBRN) allocated to the appointment which is the subject of the cancellation request.

The Identifier External datatype flavour is used to carry this information as follows:

  • The root attribute shall contain an OID with the value 2.16.840.1.113883.2.1.3.2.4.1
  • The extension attribute shall contain the UBRN
