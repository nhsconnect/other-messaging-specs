PORX_RM135001UK32
CancellationRequest

This message is sent by an authorised person from the prescribing system to the ETP Component to attempt to cancel either an item on a previously sent prescription, or the whole prescription, so that it is not dispensed.

Reference: W5-5.4.2.1
