Description

Default Scheme: NHS Data Dictionary ReligiousAffiliation

Assigned OID: 2.16.840.1.113883.2.1.3.2.4.16.21

Based on HL7 v3 Religious Affiliation with two additions.

A religion of which a person regards him or her self as an adherent, for example Anglican, Jehovah's Witness, Sunni Moslem, Bahai.
