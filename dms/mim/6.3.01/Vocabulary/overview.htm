1    Overview

A cross-index showing the usage of each vocabulary in HL7 v3 clinical messages appears in the vocabulary cross reference file.

 

The human readable display name of any transmitted SNOMED CT concept shall be the preferred term (or an authorised synonym) for the concept as it is displayed in the UK Edition of SNOMED CT and shall retain the case format, spacing and special characters of the term.
