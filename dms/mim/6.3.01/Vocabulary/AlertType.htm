Vocabulary for AlertType

Version: 2.0
Status: active
Date: 2007-03-09

Description

A generic vocabulary acting as a place holder for a number of specific codes which identify the alert type codes.  The codes may come from definitions found in the MIM or may be defined externally.

SealedEnvelopes (OID = 2.16.840.1.113883.2.1.3.2.4.17.199)
The concepts identifying alert types in Sealed Envelope Alerts are defined externally by the Sealed Envelope Team.

Dissent (OID = 2.16.840.1.113883.2.1.3.2.4.17.201)
The concepts identifying alert types for dissents.  These are defined in the MIM vocabulary DissentAlertType.
