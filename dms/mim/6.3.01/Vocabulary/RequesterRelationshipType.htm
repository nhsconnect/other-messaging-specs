Vocabulary for RequesterRelationshipType

Code System Id

2.16.840.1.113883.2.1.3.2.4.17.162

Version: 3.0
Status: active
Date: 2007-11-08

Description

Indicates the relationship of the person to the patient when sealing of information has been requested.

Values

Code Description Note
1 Parent/Legal Guardian of child

Parent/Legal Guardian of child
2 Clinician on behalf of an adult

Responsible Clinician making a decision on behalf of an adult who lacks the capacity to do so.
3 Clinician on behalf of a child

Responsible Clinician making a decision on behalf of a child where the Parent/Legal Guardian is unknown, cannot be contacted or also lacks capacity
4 Power of Attorney

Person exercising Lasting Power of Attorney
5 Court Appointed

Person appointed by court to make a decision on behalf of anotherperson.
0 Patient
