Version: 3.0
Status: active
Date: 2005-03-22

Description

Represent the role of a person in relation to the patient. The list is non-exhaustive and systems need not support all the detailed concepts specified in a coded representation. However, it is essential to distinguish between information about the patient and information about another party.
