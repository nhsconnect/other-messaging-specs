If this is the request to dispense, then it may be open ended.

You can give patients scripts to get if they need them - common

when people have recurrent bladder infections for example. In

this case they take the script to the pharmacy only if they get

an infection.

 

However, scripts do expire - I think the statutory limit is 6

months after they are dated. (need to check that) so there is

always an upper limit in reality.

 

I think it would be impossible to give a shorter limit than the

statutory one.

 

However, docs have been known to leave a script undated, telling

the patient to fill it in when they need it... I guess we can

ignore that.

 

Pete
