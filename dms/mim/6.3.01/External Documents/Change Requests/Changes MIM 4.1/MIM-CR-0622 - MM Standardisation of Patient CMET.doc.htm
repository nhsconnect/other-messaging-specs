Short Title of Issue

Standardisation of Patient CMET

Description

 

Bugzilla issue

 

10312

R_Patient-Patient-SourceOf. Which HL7 vocabulary is the value Registered. With taken from? It appears to be made up.

Perceived Impact

The proposed change will require the creation of a new version of the R_Patient CMET.  This will impact all of the artefacts associated with the following messages. 

·         PORX_RM114002UK31  -  PANotification
·         PORX_RM014001UK31  -  PAClaimInfo
·         PORX_RM114001UK31  -  PANotificationWithClaimInfo
·         PORX_RM024003UK31  -  PSNotification
·         PORX_RM142003UK31  -  PSNotificationWithClaimInfo
·         PORX_RM132004UK31  -  ParentPrescription
·         PORX_RM122003UK32  -  PrescriptionResponse
·         PORX_RM135001UK31  -  CancelRequest
·         PORX_RM135201UK31  -  CancellationResponse

 The change will also require updating the MM IM; updating the CMET IM and example message; and a minor update to the MM W5.

Outwith the MiM:

-          The XSLT for digital signatures will need changing (if they have already been created)

-          Mastek will have to create new (different) templates for Patient for all the messages

 

Reason for issue being raised

This CR has been raised to standardise the Patient CMET based on the PDS patient demographic structure, as the former currenty uses a non-standard demographic structure and contains an invalid typeCode value (registeredWith).  This will make any future changes that might be mandated by PDS much easier to implement within ETP. 

Suggested Action

 Propose to create a new version of the CMET as shown, and have the above messages reference it.
