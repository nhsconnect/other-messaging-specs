Short Title of Issue

Author of Discharge

 

Description

Unable to represent an HCP who is responsible for discharge, but not the author of the ‘Discharge’ message.

 

Suggested Action

Add author participation with relevant CMET(s) attached to the Discharge class in the ‘Discharge’ message.
