Presenting Information will be used to describe the presenting problem of the patient on arrival, it will comprise:

- Findings
- Diagnoses
- Problems and Issues
- Social Context
- Family History
- Medication History
- Risks and Warnings (including Allergies and Adverse Reactions)
- Procedures (including Investigations and Treatments)
- Personal Preferences
- Documents/Correspondence
