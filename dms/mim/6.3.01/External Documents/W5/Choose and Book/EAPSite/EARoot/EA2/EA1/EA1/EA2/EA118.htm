SDI can have five values
N = not set
Y= stop noted records which have not been assigned a catagory on CHRIS
I= Invalid - no longer active (duplicate records, identity/gender change, witness protection etc
B= Business Purposes - questionable data quality and should be treated with caution
S= Sensitive - where personal details require protection from unauthorised access, e.g. battered wives
