- Update GP Summaries with out asking me
- I always want all my GP Summary items to be excluded (Send a “blank” summary)
- I wish to discuss any new items being entered on to my summary. My preferred contact means is …
Authorised User Chooses to Record Patients Preferences Trigger Approved Authorised User Chooses to Record Patients Preferences
Record Patients Summary Creation Preferences Description Approved It is necessary to record the patients preferences for creating GP Summaries. This is so that the preference can be applied each time a new GP Summary is created.
