View Care Event Constraint
Use Case Detail
1. Goal To enable a patient to view clinical information held in their NHS care record on PSIS
2. Trigger Patient wishes to view a care event.
3. Pre-condition 1. Patient authenticated to eGif Level 3
2. Authority to access required information and legitimate relationship
4. Success Post-condition Patient successfully views information as available in the NHS care record, according to the criteria corresponding to the relevant view.
5. Guaranteed Post-condition If the user is not able to view the data, a page is displayed describing the error.
