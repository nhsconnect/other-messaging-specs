A Uniform Resource Identifier used to identify any of points of content, such as a page of text, a media file, a program, etc.

Types of URI include URL's (Uniform Resource Locators), used to identify resources on computer systems or networks; and URN's (Uniform Resource Names) that define an institutional namespaces for resources.

Examples: http://intranet.connectingforhealth.nhs.uk/, urn:hl7-org:v3

Notes: would typically use URL
