A Uniform Resource Locator specified according to Internet standard RFC 2396 [http://www.ietf.org/rfc/rfc2396.txt].

Common uses of the URL are for telephone and telefax numbers, e-mail addresses, Hypertext references, FTP references, etc.

Examples: 01482123456, joe.bloggs@yahoo.com, http://www.google.com

Note: use Telecom for telephone numbers, URL's etc, in preference to URL, as provides additional business attributes.

HL7 v3 equivalent: none (see Telecom)
