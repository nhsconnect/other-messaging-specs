Vocabulary for EncounterSnCT

Description

Assigned OID : 2.16.840.1.113883.2.1.3.2.4.15

A subset of the Snomed-CT clinical terminology (i.e. coding system) intended for use in the UK. Previously named SnomedCTUK that describes an encounter between a care professional and the patient (or patient's record). The patient by be represented by a third party such as a carer or family member.
