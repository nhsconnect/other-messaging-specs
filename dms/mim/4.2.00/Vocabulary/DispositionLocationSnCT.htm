Vocabulary for DispositionLocationSnCT

  

Description 

Assigned OID:   See SnomedCT

 

A subset of SnomedCT (UK) codes that represents a location that a patient might be sent to on completion of an Out of Hours or Emergency Department encounter.

eg. “Lodged patient at same trust”; “Discharged, GP follow up”;  “Referred to A&E Clinic”; “Referred to Fracture Clinic”; “Referred to OP Clinic”; “Transferred to other Trust”;  “Referred to other HCP”.
