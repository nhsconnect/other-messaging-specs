Various different views may be taken by the HCP during the course of the consultation. In logical process terms, it is assumed that all information to support consultation is collated at this point, including retrieval from PSIS. Obviously the majority of health care records are too large to be viewed on one screen and systems commonly divide information between screens based on a number of criteria including problem orientated views, service type (Pathology, Radiology), summary views etc. For ease of reading, all accesses to these views within the remainder of this Business Process are regarded as user actions since the system has already collated such information.

Suppliers within their physical implementations may at their discretion choose to only query PSIS for detailed/filtered information when requested by the user.
