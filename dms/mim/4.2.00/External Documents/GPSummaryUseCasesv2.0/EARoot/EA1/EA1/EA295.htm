All users acting in a role of general medical practitioner will have access rights to change the PDS consent flag
This should be part of the baseline RBAC policy throughout NCRS

Each NHS organisation will decide who in their organisation will be trained to inform patients about NCRS consent/dissent to information sharing and its implications and record the patient’s wishes by changing the value of the PDS consent flag, and each of these users will be granted the appropriate Business Function to give them that access privilege
