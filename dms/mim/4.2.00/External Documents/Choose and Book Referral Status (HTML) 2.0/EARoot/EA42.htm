public int
  errorCode
Notes: A code indicating the nature of the error. The following errors are relevant to requests for outline and detail information.
[1] Enquirer could not be found (unique identifier invalid).
[2] Enquirer is not acting in a role that is authorised to request information about referrals.
[3] Enquirer not entitled to view this patient's referrals.
[4] Patient could not be found (NHS Number invalid).
[5] Patient is stop-noted as I, S or Y.
[6] Internal error in Choose and Book.

The following errors are only relevant to request for detailled information:
[7] Enquirer does not have reason to request information about the UBRN.
[8] UBRN does not exist
[9] NHS number and UBRN are for different patients.
[10] Enquirer has requested clinical information and is not authorised to do so.
[11] Detail of this UBRN is no longer available.
