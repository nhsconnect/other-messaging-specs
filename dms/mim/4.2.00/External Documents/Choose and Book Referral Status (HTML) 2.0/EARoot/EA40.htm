The RMIM specifies a constraint that each referralComponent must be referenced by at least one categoryHeader.

A referralComponent can be part of a compoundStatement. The compoundStatement is referenced directly by at least one categoryHeader. However, each referralComponent that is part of the compoundStatement need not be referenced by a categoryHeader individually.

The association between the structuredReferralLetter and each referralComponent that is part of a compoundStatement can be inferred from the ownership of the compoundStatement by the structuredReferralLetter.
