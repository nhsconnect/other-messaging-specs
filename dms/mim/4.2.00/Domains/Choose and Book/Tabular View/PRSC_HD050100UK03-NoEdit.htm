PRSC_RM050000UK03 
RequestedAppointment

The Request Appointment Response represents either a Confirmation or a Rejection of the request appointment confirmation sent from Choose and Book to the service provider.

A confirmation interaction confirms that the scheduled service has been booked by the service provider for the patient

A rejection interaction states that an appointment slot in which a service request has been scheduled is no longer available.
