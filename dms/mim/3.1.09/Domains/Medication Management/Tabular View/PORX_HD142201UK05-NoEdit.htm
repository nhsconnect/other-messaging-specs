PORX_RM142201UK05
ReimbursementClaimReject

This message is used by the reimbursement organisation (the PPA in P1R2) to notify a dispensing contractor that a request for reimbursement for a dispensed prescription has been rejected, with reason(s) for the rejection.

Reference: W5 Section 3.4.7.1
