PORX_RM142202UK05
ReimbursementClaimReject

This message is used by the Reimbursement Organisation to notify a dispensing contractor that a request for reimbursement for a Protocol Supply has been rejected, giving the reason(s) for the rejection.

Reference: W5-14.4.8.2
