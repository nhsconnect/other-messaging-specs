Vocabulary for DissentOverride

  

Description:

Assigned OID:      2.16.840.1.113883.2.1.3.2.4.17.60 

A code indicating whether or not dissent to access a patient's NHS Care Record should be overriden, and if so, the reason for it.  This vocabulary is a combination of previous vocabularies: DissentOverrideIndicator and DissentOverrideReasonCode.

 

Values:

Version:

01

Date:

29th October 2004

 

Code

Description

Note
0 No override

 
1 Access made in the public interest

 
2 Access required by statute

 
3 Access required by Court Order  
4 Access made in the best interests of a patient who now lacks the mental capacity to give consent, either temporarily (because, for example, they are unconscious) or permanently  
5 Access made in the best interests of a non-competent child patient when the parent or guardian of the child has refused to give consent, and where there is insufficient time to obtain a court order.
