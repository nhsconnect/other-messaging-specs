A code indicating what kind of information this CompoundStatement contains. It affects the vocabulary applicable to the EhrCompoundCode of the same CompoundStatement, q.v..  The codes are taken from the Read Codes Version 2.

 Code

Description

Note

9b40.

Topic

A subdivision of a composition related to a particular clinical issue (such as a problem)

E.g. that part of the consultation concerned with the patient’s diabetes.

9b41.

Category

A subdivision of a Composition or Topic containing similar elements (e.g. Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan)

E. g, the history of the presenting complaint as described by the patient during the consultation

9b42.

Cluster

A subdivision of a Composition, Topic or Category containing closely related Statements and / or Batteries

E.g. a series of blood pressure readings, or a full blood count and ESR 

9b43.

Battery

A subdivision of Composition, Topic, Category  or Cluster containing Statements that comprise the observations from an identifiable investigation.

E.g. the results obtained from a single blood sample or blood pressure observation
