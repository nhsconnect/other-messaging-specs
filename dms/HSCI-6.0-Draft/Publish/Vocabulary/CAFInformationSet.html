CAFInformationSet

Code SystemId: 2.16.840.1.113883.2.1.3.2.4.17.222
Version: 2.0
Status: active
Date: 2007-12-03

Description

This vocab consists of 3 levels of classification. The 7 Outcomes for adults come from the white paper Our Health, Our Care, Our Say. These have then been broken down into groups of domains, which breakdown further into sub-domains. This vocab is consistent with version 2.0 of the CAF information set produced by the Department of Health.
