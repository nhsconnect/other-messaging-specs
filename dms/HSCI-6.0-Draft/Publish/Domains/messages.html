Assessment Notice (COMT_MT000002UK03)

The Assessment Notice is required to be sent under the Care Act 2014, schedule 3, para (1) (1) to notify social services of a patient's likely need for community care services after discharge. This Assessment Notice replaces the previous assessment notification Section 2 under the Community Care (Delayed Discharge) Act 2003.The payload of an Assessment Notice contains Assessment Notice data, a renderable CDA version of the Assessment Notice and optionally a Health and Social Care Contact Assessment.

Withdrawal Notice (COMT_MT000005UK03)

The Withdrawal Notice is required to be sent under the Care Act 2014 schedule 3, para (1) (1) to notify social services of the withdrawal of a patient's notice. This could be withdrawing either the assessment or discharge notice or both the assessment and discharge notices as appropriate. This Withdrawal Notice replaces the previous withdrawal notification Section 3 under the Community Care (Delayed Discharge) Act 2003. There is a corresponding 'acknowledgement' response from Social Services.
